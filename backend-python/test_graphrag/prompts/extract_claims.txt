-Goal-
Extract factual claims from the text with Islamic scholarship standards.

-Steps-
1. Identify factual claims about:
   - Historical events (battles, migrations, treaties)
   - Family relationships (marriages, parentage)
   - Religious concepts and teachings
   - Hadith transmissions and authenticity
   - Biographical facts (ages, dates, locations)

2. For each claim, extract:
   - subject: The entity the claim is about
   - object: What is claimed about the subject
   - claim_type: One of [fait_historique, relation_familiale, enseignement_religieux, transmission_hadith, fait_biographique, date_evenement]
   - description: Full statement of the claim
   - source_type: Coran, Hadith (specify authenticity), Sira, Tafsir
   - source_reference: Specific citation if available (e.g., "Bukhari 5133")
   - confidence: high (explicit in text) / medium (strongly implied) / low (inferred)

-CRITICAL for Islamic content-
- Always note Hadith authenticity level (Sahih, Hassan, Daif)
- Distinguish between established facts and scholarly opinions
- Note if multiple sources confirm the claim
- Flag any contradictions between sources
